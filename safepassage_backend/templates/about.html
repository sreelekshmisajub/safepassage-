SafePassage India

SafePassage India is the first AI safety ecosystem designed specifically for the dynamics of Indian urban mobility, from the crowded lanes of Old Delhi to the tech corridors of Bangalore.

Our Mission

Enhance personal safety in Indian cities through predictive intelligence. We leverage local crime patterns and cultural data to protect the most vulnerable commuters.

Our Vision

A 100% safe urban India powered by AI. We envision a future where every citizen and tourist can move through any Indian city at any hour with a reliable digital guardian.

India-Centric Technology

Adaptive algorithms that understand the unique temporal and spatial crime patterns of the Indian subcontinent.

NCRB Data Modeling

Models trained on National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) statistics and state-specific FIR data for high-resolution risk mapping.

Localization Engines

Support for regional dialects and Indian cultural protocols in tourist zones.

Hinglish NLP

Natural Language Processing optimized for Indian English, Hindi, and local slang for distress detection.

Modes of Intelligence

Comprehensive protection systems tailored for specific urban roles in India.

FOR COMMUTERS & WORKERS

Night Worker Mode

designed to protect workers commuting during high-risk nighttime hours by combining real-time risk monitoring, safe navigation, and emergency alert systems.

Active Monitoring

Access the Night Worker Dashboard for Route Risk Scores, Safe Havens, and Employer Check-in Status. Continuously analyzes crime patterns, lighting, and CCTV zones.

Safe Route Planner

Generates the safest route—not just the shortest—prioritizing well-lit streets, CCTV-covered areas, and active commercial zones.

Automated Check-ins

Timed safety checks ensure presence and safety. Failure to confirm triggers an alert to the employer with live GPS coordinates.

Emergency Panic

One-tap Panic Button shares live location with dispatchers and contacts, and includes Voice SOS activation for high-stress moments.

FOR TRAVELERS & EXPLORERS

Tourist Mode

Empowering tourists to explore India with confidence by bridging cultural gaps and providing proactive security layers.

Multilingual Guardian

A dedicated Cultural Guardian providing Do's, Don'ts, and Scam Alerts in five regional Indian languages plus English.

Vicinity Intelligence

A dynamic Risk Map that detects your GPS location and identifies nearby crime hotspots, crowd surges, or weather alerts.

Regional Phrasebook

Direct access to emergency phrases translated across Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, and English for immediate local communication.

SOS Dispatch

Instant loud or silent SOS modes that send live Google Maps links to the SafePassage India Global Dispatch Center.
